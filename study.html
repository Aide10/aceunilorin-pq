Study Tips
⏱️ Short sessions win. Study for 20–30 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Repeat.
✍️ Test yourself. After reading notes, close them and try to recall key points before doing quizzes.
📅 Rotate subjects. Don't study one subject all day — switching keeps your brain fresh.
🎯 Review mistakes. Wrong answers teach you more than correct ones. Re-read explanations carefully.
